About Us

Caught On Cam is a lifestyle, portrait and documentary photographer from Somerset who now lives and works in London. Starting career in fashion photography at the age of 18, it was on the sets of high end brand campaigns and fashion shows learnt the technicalities of what has become signature style. Having always been drawn to sociological documentary photography, it applies classic and energetic style seamlessly to portraiture and documentary project
